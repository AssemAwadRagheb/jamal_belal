SYSTEM PROFILE

JAMAL BELAL

Jamal Belal is a Syrian-Turkish music producer and composer specializing in hard techno, dubstep, and electronic music. With a background in music production since the age of 15, he has developed a unique sound that combines industrial energy with precision-tooled percussion.

Born on July 27, 1995, with origins from Damascus and Adana, Jamal currently resides in Istanbul, Turkey. He holds a music production certificate from Royal Schools of Music and has produced over 12 albums and 1500 royalty-free tracks.

His music has been featured in various media, including the Arabic series "Black Day", and he continues to push boundaries in the hard techno scene with his relentless productions.
