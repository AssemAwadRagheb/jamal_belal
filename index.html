My Journey Through Sound

2010 - The Genesis

Began my musical journey at age 15, composing and producing electronic music using FruityLoops (FL Studio), laying the foundation for my sonic explorations.

2015 - Formal Training & Certification

Achieved a prestigious music production certificate from Royal Schools of Music, complementing my studies at the esteemed Damascus Music Institute.

2018 - Istanbul & Professional Launch

Relocated to Istanbul, Turkey, marking a pivotal moment in my career as I established my professional music production studio and brand.

2020 - "Black Dragon Fury" Album Release

Unleashed the critically acclaimed album "Black Dragon Fury," a 9-track masterpiece blending electronic and dubstep styles, gaining significant traction.

2024-2025 - Global Recognition & Hit Singles

Released multiple chart-topping singles including "Riot," "Flute Step," and "Rage," solidifying my presence and gaining international recognition in the electronic music scene.

Present - Prolific Creator & Industry Contributor

To date, I have proudly produced over 12 full-length albums and contributed 1500+ royalty-free music tracks, widely utilized in diverse media projects across the globe.
