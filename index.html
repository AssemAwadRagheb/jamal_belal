System Profile

Jamal Belal

Jamal Belal is a Syrian-Turkish music producer and composer specializing in Hard Techno, Dubstep, and Electronic Music. With a background in music production since the age of 15, he has developed a unique sound that combines industrial energy with precise rhythms.

Born on July 27, 1995, with Damascus and Adana origins, he currently resides in Istanbul, Turkey. He holds a degree in Music Production from the Royal Schools of Music and has produced over 12 albums and 1500 royalty-free music tracks.

His music has been featured in various media, including the Arabic series "Black Day," and he continues to push boundaries in the hard techno scene with his relentless productions.
